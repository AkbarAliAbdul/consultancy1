Our Empowerment Programs

Comprehensive programs for economic and social empowerment

Vocational Training

Skill development programs in tailoring, embroidery, handicrafts, and traditional arts for sustainable livelihoods.

  • 6-month certified courses
  • Job placement assistance
  • Toolkit provision
  • Market linkage support

Digital Skills

Digital literacy and technical training for youth to access employment opportunities in the digital economy.

  • Basic computer training
  • Digital marketing courses
  • Graphic design training
  • Data entry operations

Entrepreneurship

Business development support, micro-finance linkage, and mentorship for aspiring entrepreneurs.

  • Business plan development
  • Micro-loan facilitation
  • Mentorship programs
  • Market access support

Leadership Development

Building leadership skills, confidence, and community engagement among women and youth.

  • Leadership workshops
  • Public speaking training
  • Community organizing
  • Social awareness programs

Legal Awareness

Education on legal rights, gender justice, and access to legal support for women.

  • Women's rights workshops
  • Legal aid clinics
  • Domestic violence awareness
  • Property rights education

Health & Wellness

Comprehensive health education, reproductive health awareness, and wellness programs.

  • Women's health camps
  • Mental wellness workshops
  • Nutrition education
  • Stress management training

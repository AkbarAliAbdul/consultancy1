Our Education Programs

Comprehensive educational support for every child

School Support Program

Financial assistance for school fees, uniforms, books, and transportation for underprivileged children.

  • School fee sponsorship
  • Uniform and shoe distribution
  • Book bank and library access
  • Transportation assistance

Digital Literacy

Bridging the digital divide through computer education and access to technology in rural areas.

  • Computer learning centers
  • Basic digital skills training
  • Internet access facilities
  • Coding workshops for youth

Scholarship Program

Merit-based scholarships for talented students from economically weak backgrounds.

  • High school scholarships
  • College fee support
  • Professional course funding
  • Competitive exam preparation

After-School Support

Tuition classes, homework help, and skill development programs for academic excellence.

  • Evening tuition centers
  • Homework assistance
  • Exam preparation support
  • Remedial classes

Learning Resources

Providing educational materials, books, and learning aids to enhance the learning experience.

  • Educational kit distribution
  • Library establishment
  • Science lab equipment
  • Educational toys for young children

Career Guidance

Counseling and guidance for higher education and career opportunities for youth.

  • Career counseling sessions
  • College application support
  • Skill assessment tests
  • Industry exposure visits

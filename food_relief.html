Our Food & Relief Programs

Comprehensive support to combat hunger and provide emergency relief

Daily Meal Program

Providing hot, nutritious meals to underprivileged children, elderly, and homeless individuals daily.

  • Community kitchens
  • School meal programs
  • Elderly nutrition support
  • Mobile food vans

Monthly Ration Kits

Distributing essential food supplies to economically weak families on a monthly basis.

  • Rice, wheat, and pulses
  • Cooking oil and spices
  • Nutrition supplements
  • Hygiene kits included

Disaster Relief

Rapid response teams providing immediate food and essential supplies during natural disasters.

  • Flood relief operations
  • Cyclone emergency response
  • Earthquake relief camps
  • Medical aid with food support

Child Nutrition

Special nutrition programs for malnourished children with regular health monitoring.

  • Growth monitoring
  • Therapeutic food supplements
  • Nutrition education for mothers
  • Regular health checkups

Food Rescue

Collecting surplus food from restaurants and events to distribute to those in need.

  • Hotel surplus collection
  • Event food redistribution
  • Food safety training
  • Cold chain management

Food Security

Promoting sustainable food production through kitchen gardens and community farming.

  • Kitchen garden training
  • Seed distribution
  • Community farming initiatives
  • Food preservation workshops
